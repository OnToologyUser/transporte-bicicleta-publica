Vocabulary to represent the information of the public bicycle system that includes the dock stations, the bicycles, the docks, the routes with their crossing points and the users of the system. This vocabulary has been developed in the context of the "Open, collaborative and interoperable government platform" project, in which the city councils of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Compostela and A Coru√±a participate. The vocabulary reuses terms from the vocabularies [geosparql](http://www.opengis.net/doc/IS/geosparql/1.0), [schema.org](http://schema.org/) and [Dublin Core](http://purl.org/dc/terms/) which include concepts and relationships that are used in similar settings. Additionally, it refers to the [sosa](http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/), vocabulary in order to observe a bicycle station.
